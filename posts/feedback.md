**Feedback Loop**
Created a **simple**, **reusable** survey form to satisfy the need to collect feedback without overburdening the user with complexity.

- **MUI** technologies utilized include: 
    - Theme toggle 
    - Snackbar 
    - Cards 
    - Accordion 
    - Alerts 
    - Menu 
    - Paper
    - Box 
    - Container 
    - Stack
